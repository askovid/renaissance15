IET DTU presents RENAISSANCE 2015

Renaissance refers to the transitional movement in Europe that bought about a revival of art, literature and the beginnings of modern science. Most importantly, there was a shift from theoretical science to a new experimental and observational science.

With an aim to bring about a revival of sceience and technology at DTU, the IET DTU Student Chapter presents its annual techno-management fest, Renaissance'15. It is a four day event which witnesses a shift from the boring lectures and theoretical knowledge to testing how far we have come in terms of our practical prowess in a playing field which gets participation from all over the country.

With events from the field of electrical, Embedded systems, robotics, civil, mechanical, management as well as other brain booster events, there is enough to keep everyone entertained for the four days and still asking for more.

Robotic Events

  • ROBO RALLY

    DAY 1
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Robo Rally
    Mechanical bot is used in this competition. The objective of the bot is to cross the different hurdles in the entire track ,in minimum time. Hurdles can be of different types like bottles, cloth bridge, inclined plane, rotating disk, fire etc. entire track is designed and set up involving all these hurdles.

  • ROBO SOCCER

    DAY 1
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Robo Soccer
    Mechanical bot is used for the event.Two mech bots are made to play agains each other, each one has to move the ball past the net of the opponents side (similar to soccer except that it is a 2 player game).


  • LINE FOLLOWING ROBOT

    DAY 2
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Line Following Robot
    autonomous bot is used for this competition. The objective is to follow the line and complete the entire track in minimum time with least deviation from the original track. Usually the track is of black color and background is white, but the background and track color may change.

  • ROBO MASSACRE

    DAY 2
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Robo Massacref
    Involves heavy weighed and highly sophicticated mechanical bots of extremely intelligent and concrete designs .Two bots are made to fight against each other. The objective of the bot is to turn the other bot upside down.

  • QUIZMOZ

    DAY 1
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Quizmoz
    The general knowledge based quiz . Usually the questions are from the current affairs. The questions may be indirect and twisted to test the general aptitude of the participants.


  • ANTICHESS

    DAY 2
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Antichess
    Sometimes,you have to lose everything to emerge victorious.The concept is undoubtedly appropriate for this event.Loser's chess is a popular variation of chess.The motive here is -The first contestant to lose all his chess pieces wins.

  • MUS

    DAY 2
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Spelliarmus
    Also called ‘spelling bee’ . the participant is asked to spell some of the most difficult and complex words in the dictionary. It also involves testing the vocabulary and grammatical strength of the participant.

SOURCE CODE

  • BLIND CODING

    DAY 1
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Blind Coding
    The participant has to code according to a problem statement, subject to a condition that as he types a new line of code, all the other previous lines would be made invisible to him and penalty will be imposed for reviewing the previous lines of the code.

  • GAME CODING

    DAY 2
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Coding
    Two participants have to compete against each other, the participant who codes correctly and faster than the other will proceed to next round.

ELECTRIFY

  • FABRICACCION

    DAY 1
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Fabricaccion
    This event involves PCB (printed circuit board) designing. The software used is eagle cadsoft, used to design the complex electronic circuits to, which is finally embedded into a PCB.




  • SPARK-E

    DAY 2
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Spark-E
    The first round involves quiz related to basics of electrical and electronics engineering. The second round involves making some electronic hardware prototype based on the problem statement specified e.g. the participants may be asked to assemble any analog circuit on perfboard through soldering

  • TECH-X-BIT

    DAY 3
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Tech-X-Bit
    The technical paper presentation event held every year witnesses a large participation of detailed,quality research papers on a plethora of topics from the domains of various fields of engineering.



BRIDGE AND GEARS

  • CONTRAPTION

    DAY 2
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Contraption
    It is an entire new concept based on the ‘domino effect’. An apparatus is being designed wherein all the components move one after the other in synchronism, only after the first component has been manually displaced. The team which makes the perfect prototype with perfect display of domino effect wins.

  • BRIDGING THE GAP

    DAY 3
    VENUE:
    PROBLEM STATEMENT

    Bridging The Gap
    The objective of the participants is to make a model of a bridge (of roadways) using ice cream sticks. The design of the bridge can range from simple ones to more sophisticated designs.

IET-DTU Student Chapter

It is one of the leading IET student chapter organisations in India. The main objective of this largely student-run body is to provide a convergence platform for the exchange of technical ideas. The college chapter also organises a number of workshops , lecture sessions and contests throughout the year; the most notable being the tech week, “Armageddon” and the annual tech fest,”Renaissance”.
